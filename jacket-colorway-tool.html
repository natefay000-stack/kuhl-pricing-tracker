How it works
On upload, an on-device ML model (SegFormer, clothing-parsing) isolates the jacket from the background, mannequin, and model. Smart Click uses edge-aware flood fill to capture the fabric you click. Each masked pixel keeps its original brightness, so shading, folds, and stitches survive the recolor. Brush / Erase / Grow / Shrink clean up edges when needed. The designer is the detector: you know which panels matter for your tech pack.
